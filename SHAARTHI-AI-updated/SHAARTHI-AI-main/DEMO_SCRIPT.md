## **Q&A Talking Points**

### **Q: How is Saarthi different from ChatGPT?**
> "ChatGPT is general-purpose. Saarthi is **hyper-localized** with:
> - Scam detection (ChatGPT doesn't have this)
> - Government scheme knowledge (India-specific)
> - Voice-first design for non-English speakers
> - 4 distinct personas, not one generic chatbot"

### **Q: How do you monetize this?**
> "Freemium model:
> - Basic features free
> - Premium tier (₹99/month) for advanced AI features
> - B2B: Sell to NGOs, government, financial institutions
> - Government contracts for digital literacy"

### **Q: Scalability concerns?**
> "We're using:
> - Microservices architecture
> - MongoDB for horizontal scaling
> - Vercel for auto-scaling
> - Caching layers for optimization
> - Ready for 100M users"

### **Q: Why focus on scams?**
> "Because **scams are the #1 reason Indians distrust digital systems.** When we solve this, we unlock 1 billion new users for the digital economy."
